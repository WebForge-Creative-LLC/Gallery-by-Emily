About Emily

The artist behind the colorful creations

Hello, I'm Emily

Welcome to my colorful world! I'm an artist passionate about bringing joy and vibrancy to everyday life through my artwork.

My artistic journey began when I was a child, always drawn to bright colors and expressive forms. My true education has been through years of experimentation, travel, and observing the beauty in everyday moments.

I founded Gallery by Emily in January 2025 as a way to share my creations with a wider audience. What started as a personal passion has blossomed into a business that allows me to connect with art lovers everywhere.

My Artistic Vision

My work is characterized by bold, vibrant colors and joyful expressions. I believe art should evoke emotion and create connections ‚Äì whether it's a feeling of happiness, nostalgia, or simply a moment of peace in our busy lives.

I'm inspired by the natural world, human connections, and the interplay of light and color. Each piece I create tells a story and captures a moment or feeling that I hope resonates with viewers.

My goal is to create art that not only beautifies spaces but also uplifts spirits and sparks conversations. I believe that art is essential to our wellbeing and that everyone deserves to have pieces that speak to them personally.

Materials & Techniques

I work with a variety of materials and techniques to create diverse artistic expressions. My primary mediums include:

Acrylic Paint

I love the versatility and vibrant colors of acrylic paints, which allow for both bold statements and delicate details.

Watercolor

For more fluid, dreamy pieces, I turn to watercolors which create beautiful translucent effects.

Mixed Media

I often incorporate various elements like paper, fabric, and found objects to add texture and dimension.
